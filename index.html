ViSCALE Workshop | CVPR2025

Introduction

The Test-time Scaling for Computer Vision (ViSCALE) workshop at CVPR2025 aims to explore the application of test-time scaling techniques in computer vision, inspired by recent advancements in large language models (LLMs). We will focus on challenges, opportunities, and future directions of scaling models during inference, with a special emphasis on multimodal models, generative models, and novel algorithmic solutions. Topics covered will include theoretical analysis, efficiency improvements, robustness and safety, and more.

Call for Papers

We invite high-quality papers on the following topics:

  • Theoretical analysis of test-time scaling in computer vision.
  • Suitability of test-time scaling in traditional computer vision tasks.
  • Extensions of test-time scaling to multimodal foundation models and generative models.
  • Efficient algorithms for vision models with test-time scaling.
  • Trustworthiness issues like robustness, safety, and interpretability.
  • Novel challenges posed by test-time scaling in computer vision.
  • Evaluations and benchmarks on test-time scaling techniques.

For submission details and formatting instructions, please refer to the Submission Instructions.
